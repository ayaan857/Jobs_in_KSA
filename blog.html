Jobs & Salaries in Saudi Arabia 2025 – Complete Guide

Saudi Arabia continues to attract workers from Pakistan, India, Bangladesh, Nepal, and other countries due to its tax-free income, high demand for skilled and unskilled labor, and safe working environment. This blog provides a detailed guide on job types, salaries, cost of living, and tips for expats.

1. Why Saudi Arabia is a Top Destination for Workers

  • High demand for foreign workers
  • Tax-free salary
  • Safe and regulated working environment
  • Career growth opportunities
  • Benefits like accommodation, transport, and medical insurance

3. Factors Affecting Salary

  • Company size and industry type
  • Experience and skill level
  • City location (Riyadh & Jeddah typically pay more)
  • Working hours, shifts, and overtime opportunities

4. Cost of Living in Saudi Arabia (2025)

  • Food: 250–450 SAR/month
  • Shared Room: 300–600 SAR/month
  • Transport: 80–150 SAR/month
  • Mobile & Internet: 50–100 SAR/month

Providing accommodation and transport by the employer can save 40–60% of expenses.

5. Who Should Move to Saudi Arabia?

Ideal candidates:

  • Workers seeking tax-free income
  • People wanting to upgrade skills
  • Those seeking a safe and stable work environment
  • Individuals looking to save money for family/future

Not suitable if expecting luxury lifestyle on entry-level jobs, or quick promotions without proper skills.

6. Tips for Workers Coming to Saudi Arabia

  • Learn a basic skill before arriving
  • Improve English for better job opportunities
  • Apply only via verified agents or companies
  • Confirm contract details (salary, accommodation, transport, food)
  • Understand iqama rules and avoid illegal work
  • Plan your budget based on real living costs

7. Final Thoughts

This guide, along with the video, provides a realistic picture of jobs, salaries, and living costs in Saudi Arabia for 2025. Proper planning and research help expats maximize savings and secure the right job opportunity.
